There are several ways to save a link for your convenience. Just one or two clicks away to save any links on any of your devices.

## Save at Brace.to

Click the '+ add' button on our website.

<Image className="mx-auto dark:hidden" src={saveLinkAtTheSite} alt="" priority={true} />
<Image className="hidden mx-auto dark:block" src={saveLinkAtTheSiteDark} alt="" priority={true} />

## Save in Address Bar

Type 'brace.to' and '/' in front of any link in the web browser address bar.

<Image className="mx-auto dark:hidden" src={saveLinkInUrlBar} alt="" />
<Image className="hidden mx-auto dark:block" src={saveLinkInUrlBarDark} alt="" />

## Save with our Extension

Install our [Chrome](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/brace/hennjddhjodlmdnopaggbjjkpokpbdnn) or [Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/brace/) web browser extension and click on our extension icon next to the address bar.

<Image className="mx-auto dark:hidden" src={saveWithExtension} alt="" />
<Image className="hidden mx-auto dark:block" src={saveWithExtensionDark} alt="" />

## Save via our Mobile App

Install our app from the [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.bracedotto) or [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/id1531456778) on your device and share a link with it.
